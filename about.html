Our Story

Niyaz’s institute of Commerce and Economics has been started in 2001 by Advocate Niyaz Pathan.

The promoter was CA Final Students during 2001 and use to travel All the way From Uran to Mumbai for his education Training and Further studies daily. Travelling to Mumbai was time consuming and costly and it was difficult to manage studies properly. During his struggle for study and travel for education, he realised that there is heavy demand of quality educational institution in his locality and hence he decided to set up an institute in the locality so that the students good quality education at affordable cost at their door step.

With the immense service motive, he started training a small group of students and gradually the institute is grown over the years to the best institute of Uran for the students of F.Y.J.C(Com) & S.Y.J.C(Com), B. Com, B.A. F, B.M. S, M. Com, C.A, and C.S.

Now the institute is moving ahead on the success track and has distinguished itself by setting up a new standard in the world of competitive education. It is an Institution of knowledge under the guidance of Advocate Niyaz Pathan for imparting the quality education for the students. Our qualified and experienced teachers conduct coaching classes, at convenient timings. For Students is structured on the principle of consistency and hard work and believes in scientific approach based on perfect planning and proper guidance. Student’s overall development and splendid success is our prime concern. Genius teaches all subjects under one roof.

The limited strength of students in each class, its unique working schedule, pre-planning of complete syllabus, and its strict implementation, personal attention to every student, perfect working pattern, sincere and devout teachers, regular Unit-Tests, Board Pattern-wise preparation of every question paper, etc. are in the character of the institute.
